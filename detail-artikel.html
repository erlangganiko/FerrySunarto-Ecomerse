Ferry's Enchanted World

In celebration of the end-of-year festivities, the House unveils a witty film that stages, with a touch of mischief, an enchanting stroll through a maze.

The world of Ferry Sunarto and its various realms are playfully unveiled one after the other in an experience animated by the joy of giving, surprising, and inspiring dreams.

Silhouettes guide us like exquisite muses through the labyrinthine twists and turns of a fabulous garden. From there, the archives of our heritage are revealed by magic mirrors, and also a magnificent banquet is bathed in the talismanic light of the House's lucky charms. Like spellbinding apparitions, couture creations come to life, from ready-to-wear to accessories and jewelry.
